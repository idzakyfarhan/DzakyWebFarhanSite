Omu Rice by Jiwan Cibubur

Omurice or omu-rice (オムライス, Omu-raisu) is a dish served by the chef in Jiwan Cibubur consisting of an omelette made with fried rice and thin, fried scrambled eggs, usually topped with ketchup.It is a popular dish also commonly cooked at Jiwan. Children in particular enjoy omurice. It is another version of Japan children's meal, okosama-ranchi (お子様ランチ).

Jiwan Cibubur itself is located near exit Cimanggis toll or near exit of Citra Gran.

Pecel Pincuk Bu Ida

Pecel is a traditional Javanese salad with peanut sauce, usually eaten with carbs (steamed rice, lontong or ketupat).The simplicity of pecel preparation and its cheap price have contributed to its popularity throughout Java. It has become a food that represents practicality, simplicity, and travel, since the dish is often found along the train journey across Java.

Pecel Pincuk Bu Ida located in many locations spread all of Cibubur, you can find it in Alternatif Cibubur and Kota Wisata.
